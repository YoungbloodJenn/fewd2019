About Go Bag Builder

Hi, I'm Jenn Youngblood and I'm excited to welcome you to Go Bag Builder! Go Bag Builder is a project I created as part of General Assembly's Front End Web Development course in 2019. I have wanted to create a tool that helps individuals prepare for emergencies and disasters for years. As a former EMT and USN corpsman I have always had a passion for emergency/disaster prep and my training has given me the skills and knowledge to help others.

I experienced my own disaster scenario in high school when my family home was struck by lightening and burned down. It was an incident that planted a deep need in me to always be ready for the unexpected. Knowing people are prepared to "grab and go" gives me great satisfaction. In the new reality of extreme weather disasters we need to prepare individuals to respond to emergency now more than ever.
