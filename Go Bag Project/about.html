About Go Bag Builder

Hi and welcome you to Go Bag Builder! Go Bag Builder is a project I created as part of General Assembly's Front End Web Development course in 2019. I wanted to create a tool that helps individuals prepare for emergencies and disasters. As a former EMT and US Navy Corpsman I have always had a passion for emergency/disaster prep and my training has given me the skills and knowledge to help others.

My family lived through a house fire when I was I was in high school planted a deep need in me to always be ready for the unexpected. Living through 9/11 and then Hurricane Katrina & Rita further reinforced my desire to be READY! Knowing people are prepared to "grab and go" gives me great satisfaction. In the new reality of extreme weather disasters we need to prepare individuals to respond to emergency now more than ever.
